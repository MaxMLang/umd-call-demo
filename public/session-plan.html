Breakout Session Plan

Objective:

Explore new and reimagined ideas in using AI chatbots for qualitative interviews and survey data collection.

Introduction & Objectives (10 minutes)

  • Opening Remarks
  • Welcome the participants and briefly introduce yourself.
  • Outline the session's objectives: discussing AI chatbots as interviewers, exploring novel concepts, and reimagining traditional approaches to survey data collection.
  • Set Expectations:
    • Mention the two key questions for the report-out: (1) What new concept was discussed? (2) What old idea was reimagined?
    • Introduce the note takers and highlight that a summary of the breakout will be shared with attendees later.

Demo of AI Chatbot (10 minutes)

  • Live Demonstration: Provide a quick demo of an AI voice chatbot or text-based agent (from web app).
  • Encourage attendees to try it out or follow along with you.
  • Highlight key features such as dynamic probing, active listening, and personalized follow-up questions.

Overview of AI Chatbots in Research (15 minutes)

  • Discuss the shared articles on the potential of AI chatbots in research.
  • Open the floor for thoughts on the demo and its applications in attendees' fields.

Breakout Discussion (40 minutes)

  • Pose the two breakout questions to the group:
    • New Concept: What concept or idea came up in your discussion that was new or surprising to you?
    • Reimagined Idea: What traditional concept of survey interviewing was reimagined through the use of AI chatbots?
  • Encourage brainstorming and open discussion on potential AI-driven tools for their own work.

Wrap-Up (5 minutes)

  • Summarize key takeaways.
  • Reiterate the role of AI chatbots in enhancing research.
  • Remind participants that the note takers will compile and share a summary of all breakout sessions.

Thank you for participating!
